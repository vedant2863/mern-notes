NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025
by and between:

DISCLOSING PARTY: TechVentures Private Limited, a company incorporated under the
laws of India, having its registered office at 42 Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai,
Maharashtra 400051 ("Company")

RECEIVING PARTY: InnoSoft Solutions LLP, a limited liability partnership registered
under the laws of India, having its registered office at 15 Koregaon Park, Pune,
Maharashtra 411001 ("Recipient")

WHEREAS, the Company possesses certain confidential and proprietary information
relating to its business operations, technology, and strategic plans; and

WHEREAS, the Recipient desires to receive certain confidential information for the
purpose of evaluating a potential business collaboration between the parties;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the parties agree
as follows:

1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" shall mean all information, whether written, oral, or in
electronic form, disclosed by the Company to the Recipient, including but not limited
to: trade secrets, business plans, financial data, customer lists, technical
specifications, software source code, algorithms, product roadmaps, marketing
strategies, and employee information.

Confidential Information shall NOT include information that:
(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient;
(b) Was in the Recipient's possession prior to disclosure;
(c) Is independently developed by the Recipient without use of Confidential Information;
(d) Is received from a third party without restriction on disclosure.

2. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECIPIENT

The Recipient agrees to:
(a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;
(b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written
    consent of the Company;
(c) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose defined herein;
(d) Limit access to Confidential Information to employees who have a need to know;
(e) Ensure all employees who access Confidential Information sign individual NDAs;
(f) Take all reasonable measures to protect the secrecy of Confidential Information,
    using at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential
    information.

3. TERM AND DURATION

This Agreement shall be effective from the date first written above and shall remain
in effect for a period of THREE (3) years from the date of execution. The obligations
of confidentiality shall survive termination of this Agreement for an additional
period of FIVE (5) years.

4. NON-COMPETE CLAUSE

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of TWO (2) years following its
termination, the Recipient shall not directly or indirectly:
(a) Engage in any business that competes with the Company within the territory of India;
(b) Solicit or hire any employee of the Company;
(c) Approach any client or customer of the Company for competing purposes.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of the Company. This
Agreement does not grant the Recipient any license or rights to the Company's
intellectual property, patents, trademarks, or copyrights.

6. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon termination of this Agreement or upon request by the Company, the Recipient
shall promptly return or destroy all materials containing Confidential Information,
including all copies, notes, and summaries thereof, and shall provide written
certification of such destruction.

7. REMEDIES

The Recipient acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable
harm to the Company. In the event of a breach, the Company shall be entitled to
seek injunctive relief and specific performance, in addition to any other remedies
available at law or in equity, without the necessity of proving actual damages.

8. INDEMNIFICATION

The Recipient shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company from any and
all claims, losses, damages, liabilities, and expenses arising from the Recipient's
breach of this Agreement, including reasonable attorney's fees.

9. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of
India. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive
jurisdiction of the courts in Mumbai, Maharashtra.

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

The Company's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the sum of
INR 10,00,000 (Rupees Ten Lakhs only). In no event shall the Company be liable
for any indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.

11. AMENDMENT AND WAIVER

This Agreement may not be amended or modified except by a written instrument signed
by both parties. The failure of either party to enforce any provision of this
Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of such provision.

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties concerning the
subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and
negotiations.
