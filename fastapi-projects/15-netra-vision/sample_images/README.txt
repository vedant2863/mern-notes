Sample Images for Testing
=========================

To test the Netra Vision API, you can use any plant/crop photo.
Here are some suggestions:

1. healthy_tomato.jpg
   - A photo of a healthy tomato plant with green leaves
   - Expected result: severity "healthy", no diseases

2. leaf_blight.jpg
   - A photo of a plant leaf with brown spots or yellowing
   - Expected result: severity "moderate" or "severe", with disease detection

Where to find test images:
- Take a photo of any plant with your phone
- Search "crop disease images" on Google Images
- Use PlantVillage dataset (publicly available for research)
- Kaggle has several crop disease datasets with labeled images

Tips for best results:
- Clear, well-lit photos work best
- Focus on the affected area (leaf, stem, fruit)
- Include both healthy and damaged parts if possible
- JPEG or PNG format, under 5MB
